\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

\paragraph{Goal.}
This document reorganizes T1--T5 into a paper-shaped narrative:

\[
\text{definitions} \;\to\; \text{examples} \;\to\; \text{categories} \;\to\; \text{theorem}.
\]

\paragraph{Positioning (frozen).}
Rank is the source of truth; towers are reconstructed views.

\paragraph{What is included.}
We focus on the Rank lane: RankCore, the example catalog, \leaninline{RankCatLe},
and the termination kernel.

\paragraph{What is excluded (by design).}
Discussion of the older \code{Cat_*} families and probability-theory applications is deferred to Outlook.
