# Assignment 3 - Problems (Basic 5 + Challenge) — Graph Theory

## Basic Problems

### S3_P01: Adjacency in Complete Graphs
**Learning Objective**: Understand the definition of a complete graph and verify that any two distinct vertices are adjacent.
**Problem Statement**: Prove that in a complete graph Kₙ with n vertices, any two distinct vertices u and v are adjacent.
**Lean Objective**: `theorem S3_P01 (n : ℕ) (u v : Fin n) (h : u ≠ v) : (completeGraph (Fin n)).Adj u v`
**Hints**:
1. Review the definition of `completeGraph`.
2. Ensure that distinct vertices are always adjacent.

### S3_P02: Determining Bipartite Graphs
**Learning Objective**: Characterize the bipartite property of a graph through vertex partitioning.
**Problem Statement**: Prove that in a complete bipartite graph K₂,₃, the vertex set can be partitioned into two independent sets.
**Lean Objective**: `theorem S3_P02 : ∃ (A B : Set (Fin 5)), A ∪ B = Set.univ ∧ A ∩ B = ∅ ∧ (∀ u v, u ∈ A → v ∈ A → ¬(completeBipartiteGraph (Fin 2) (Fin 3)).Adj (Sum.inl u) (Sum.inl v))`
**Hints**:
1. Bipartite graphs have vertices divided into two distinct parts.
2. No two vertices in the same part are adjacent.

### S3_P03: Degree in Regular Graphs
**Learning Objective**: Comprehend the concept of a k-regular graph.
**Problem Statement**: Prove that the cycle graph C₄ with four vertices is 2-regular (all vertices have degree 2).
**Lean Objective**: `theorem S3_P03 : ∀ v : Fin 4, (cycleGraph 4).degree v = 2`
**Hints**:
1. In cycle graphs, each vertex is adjacent to its immediate neighbors.
2. This can be verified using the `decide` tactic.

### S3_P04: Center of a Star Graph
**Learning Objective**: Understand the structure and center vertex property of star graphs.
**Problem Statement**: Prove that in an n-vertex star graph Sₙ, the center vertex has a degree of n-1.
**Lean Objective**: `theorem S3_P04 (n : ℕ) (hn : 2 ≤ n) : (starGraph n).degree 0 = n - 1`
**Hints**:
1. In star graphs, vertex 0 serves as the center.
2. The center vertex is adjacent to all other vertices.

### S3_P05: Complement Graphs and Complete Graphs
**Learning Objective**: Understand the relationship between a graph and its complement.
**Problem Statement**: Prove that the complement of the empty graph is a complete graph.
**Lean Objective**: `theorem S3_P05 (V : Type*) [Fintype V] : (⊥ : SimpleGraph V)ᶜ = completeGraph V`
**Hints**:
1. The empty graph contains no edges.
2. Its complement contains all possible edges.

## 開発メモ

### 木の二部性定理（未実装）の課題
- Lean/mathlib には現時点で `G.IsTree` から直接 `G.IsBipartite` を導く補題が存在せず、自前で二部彩色を組み立てる必要があった。
- `IsTree` は「連結かつ無閉路」を意味するが、2 彩色を与える既存定理 `two_colorable_iff_forall_loop_even` を使うには「任意の閉路（Walk）が偶長」が必要で、その橋渡しに cycle / walk 分解の補題を多数挟む必要があった。
- `IsTree.Acyclic` から「奇閉路が存在しない」ことを直接示すには、Walk の `takeUntil` や `rotate` といった高度な補題を扱って閉路を単純化する作業が必要で、開発コストが高かった。
- BFS 距離にもとづく彩色を構成する案では、各連結成分で距離関数を定義し、辺で距離が 1 だけずれることを示す補題を自前で証明する必要があり、木全体に対して戦略を整理するだけでも相応の時間がかかった。
- 以上の理由から短時間で安全に証明をまとめることが難しく、現Iterationでは `K_{3,3}` から 1 辺を削除した具体例による二部性証明に切り替える判断とした。
