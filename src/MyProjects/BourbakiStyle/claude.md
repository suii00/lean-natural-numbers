# 🎓 Welcome to the Bourbaki-style Lean Proof Challenge System!

We invite you to explore the beautiful structure of mathematics and its rigorous logical foundations. This system is designed to help you develop a deep understanding of Lean proofs through the **Bourbaki-style axiomatic and structural approach**.

## 📚 Start with Basic Challenges

Select the five fundamental challenges below that best align with your interests:

### 1. **General Topology** - The Beautiful Properties of Continuous Functions
Prove that the composition of continuous functions remains continuous. This challenge will help you understand the properties of inverse images and the essential structure of open set systems.

### 2. **Algebraic Structure Theory** - The Abstract Harmony of Groups
Explore the profound relationship between quotient structures and images generated by homomorphisms through the First Isomorphism Theorem for groups.

### 3. **Order Theory** - The Mysteries of the Axiom of Choice
Work with Zorn's Lemma and examine order structures along with the existence of maximal elements. This challenge will allow you to experience the power of the Axiom of Choice.

### 4. **Measure Theory Fundamentals** - Mathematical Control Over Infinity
Learn about σ-algebras and the composition of measurable functions, understanding the fundamental structure-preserving properties of measure theory.

### 5. **Real Analysis** - The Perfect Structure of Real Numbers
Prove the completeness of the real numbers and grasp the essence of Cauchy sequences and convergence.

## 🎯 How to Progress Through Your Learning

1. **Challenge Selection**: Begin by choosing one from the above options
2. **Proof Attempt**: Try proving it using Lean
3. **Submission & Analysis**: Submit your proof for detailed analysis
4. **Next Challenges**: Based on your understanding, we'll propose three advanced pathways

## 🌟 Features of This System

- **Step-by-Step Growth**: `From axiomatic thinking → structural perspective → unified understanding`
- **Three Development Paths**: Deepening within the same field, cross-disciplinary exploration, or application integration
- **Bourbaki Spirit**: Set theory foundation, structure priority, and emphasis on universality
