## Problem Specifications

### **S2\_P01**

1. **Title**: Constructing Points on the Plane $Ax+By+Cz=D$
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to formally verify substitution into plane equations and handle division operations in Lean.
3. **Problem Statement**: Assume $C\neq 0$. For any $x,y\in\mathbb{R}$,
   prove that $P=(x,y,(D-Ax-By)/C)$ satisfies the plane equation $Ax+By+Cz=D$.
4. **Lean Goal**:

   $$
   A*x + B*y + C*((D - A*x - B*y)/C) = D
   $$
5. **Hint**: Use `field_simp [hC]` to cancel the denominator, then apply `ring`.

---

### **S2\_P02**

1. **Title**: Parameterization of a Line Passing Through Two Points
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to define the parametric equation of a line segment (parameter $t$) in Lean and demonstrate its consistency with endpoint values.
3. **Problem Statement**: For three-dimensional points $P,Q$,
   let $L(t)=P+t(Q-P)$. Prove that $L(0)=P$ and $L(1)=Q$.
4. **Lean Goal**:
   `lineParam P Q 0 = P ∧ lineParam P Q 1 = Q`
5. **Hint**: Use `ext` to separate components, then apply `simp [lineParam, sub_eq_add_neg]` followed by `ring`.

---

### **S2\_P03**

1. **Title**: Additivity of the 3D Dot Product (First Argument)
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to prove additivity through definition expansion using `simp`/`ring`.
3. **Problem Statement**: For any $u,v,w$, prove that $\mathrm{dot3}(u+w,v)=\mathrm{dot3}(u,v)+\mathrm{dot3}(w,v)$.
4. **Lean Goal**: `dot3 (u + w) v = dot3 u v + dot3 w v`
5. **Hint**: Apply `simp [dot3, add_mul]` → `ring`.

---

### **S2\_P04**

1. **Title**: Homogeneity of the 3D Dot Product (Scalar Multiplication of the First Argument)
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to handle equation expansions based on scalar multiplication definitions.
3. **Problem Statement**: For any $a\in\mathbb{R}$ and vectors $u,v$, prove that
   $\mathrm{dot3}(a\cdot u, v)=a\cdot \mathrm{dot3}(u,v)$.
4. **Lean Goal**: `dot3 (smul3 a u) v = a * dot3 u v`
5. **Hint**: Apply `simp [dot3, smul3]` followed by `ring`.

---

### **S2\_P05**

1. **Title**: Fixedness of Projection onto a Unit Vector (Parallel Lines)
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to demonstrate using the definition of projection onto a unit vector $u$ that $proj(v)=v$ when $v$ is parallel to $u$.
3. **Problem Statement**: Assume $\mathrm{dot3}(u,u)=1$ and $v=a\cdot u$. Prove that
   $\mathrm{projUnit3}(u,v)=v$.
4. **Lean Goal**: `dot3 u u = 1 → v = smul3 a u → projUnit3 u v = v`
5. **Hint**: Use P04 to obtain $\mathrm{dot3}(a u,u)=a\,\mathrm{dot3}(u,u)=a$, then substitute into the definition.

---

### **S2\_CH (Challenge)**

1. **Title**: Volume Change Under Linear Transformations is Scaled by a Factor of $|\det A|$
2. **Learning Objective**: Ability to demonstrate volume scaling using the product property of 3x3 matrix determinants and absolute value properties.
3. **Problem Statement**: Let $A\in M_{3\times3}(\mathbb{R})$, and $u,v,w\in\mathbb{R}^3$.
   Define matrix $B$ as a 3x3 matrix with $u,v,w$ as its **rows** (using `matRowsOf u v w`). Then prove:

   $$
   \left| \det( A\; B ) \right| = \left| \det A \right| \cdot \left| \det B \right|.
   $$
4. **Lean Goal**:
   `|Matrix.det (A ⬝ matRowsOf u v w)| = |Matrix.det A| * |Matrix.det (matRowsOf u v w)|`
5. **Hint**: Apply `Matrix.det_mul` and `abs_mul` using `simp`/`simpa`.
